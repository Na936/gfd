Welcome to Global Foods Distribution

Global Foods Distribution, established in 2004, is a leading importer and distributor specializing in authentic Arabian (Middle Eastern), South Asian, and Mediterranean foods. With a strong commitment to quality and tradition, the company brings a diverse range of premium products to markets worldwide, catering to both retail and foodservice sectors. Over the years, Global Foods Distribution has built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and cultural authenticity. In addition to distributing top international brands, the company proudly produces and promotes its own signature brand, Leeza Foods, known for its high-quality ingredients and genuine flavors that capture the essence of traditional cuisines.

    About GFD Limited

    Supplying Authentic Flavours from Around the World to the UK.

    Founded 2004 GFD Limited is a trusted name in food distribution, wholesale, and import-export, specializing in South Asian, Middle Eastern, and Mediterranean food products across the United Kingdom. Our mission is simple: to source, market, and deliver the highest quality, most authentic ingredients from culturally rich regions to customers throughout the UK.
    Over the years, we have grown and refined our portfolio, introducing a selection of beloved brands tailored to the diverse communities we serve.

    • Ecklee Foods: Dedicated to the Middle Eastern market, offering products celebrated for their authenticity and taste.
    • Leeza Foods: Serving the South Asian market, bringing premium ingredients and cherished traditional flavours to homes, restaurants, and retailers.
